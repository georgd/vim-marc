A plugin for transforming marc records.

To transform a record from the one-line marc format to a displayed format use the MrcCompile() function.
Change the <C-k> below to a hotkey of your choice.
map <C-k> :MrcCompile<CR>

To transfor a record from the multi-line display format to a one-line compiled format use the MrcDisplay() function.
Change the <C-l> below to a hotkey of your choice.
map <C-l> :MrcDisplay<CR>
